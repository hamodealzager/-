# Research Playbook

## Query Patterns

Use multiple query families per subtopic:

1. `"<topic>" official documentation`
2. `"<topic>" curriculum OR syllabus`
3. `"<topic>" beginner roadmap`
4. `"<topic>" advanced topics`
5. `"<topic>" project ideas OR exercises`
6. `"<topic>" best books OR open course`

## Source Prioritization

1. Official documentation and standards
2. University or educational institutions
3. Maintainer-authored guides and canonical books
4. High-quality community tutorials

## Evaluation Checklist

For each source, quickly score:

- Authority (official/reputable author)
- Freshness (publication/update date)
- Practicality (actionable for learners)
- Level fit (beginner/intermediate/advanced)

Prefer sources with strong scores in at least three criteria.
